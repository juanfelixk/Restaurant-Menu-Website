Balinese Satay Skewers

The Balinese Satay Skewers at KanSoep Bistro offer a flavorful journey into traditional Balinese cuisine with a modern twist. These tender chicken or beef skewers are marinated in a fragrant blend of coconut milk, lemongrass, and aromatic spices, then grilled to perfection for a smoky, charred finish. Served with a rich and creamy peanut sauce, each bite delivers a balance of savory, sweet, and spicy flavors. Accompanied by a fresh cucumber salad for a refreshing crunch, these skewers provide a perfect introduction to the bold tastes of Indonesia.
